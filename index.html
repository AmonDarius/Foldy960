Foldy960

A simplified and responsive 960 grid.

We've used 960.gs at Paravel for so long, it made sense to modify what we knew as we made the transition over to Responsive Web Design.

Foldy is not Yet-Another-Boilerplate. It a little kit built for those headed down a responsive route and have experience with the 960 grid. It's built to be modified.

6 is the magic number!

Foldy is a simplified 6 column grid based in spirit on the 960 grid. We also keep this as simple as possible, stripping out any prefix/suffix stuff.

Pad your content

.content-pad-left & .content-pad-right exist so you can add more padding to a block of content if needed.

.grid_right

Floating content right instead of left is helpful if you want that content to display first in the collapsed view. We have a class for that. See cat above.
